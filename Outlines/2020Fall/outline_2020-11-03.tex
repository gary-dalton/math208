\subsubsection*{The Derivative}
\begin{itemize}
	\item Secant line is the line connecting any two points on the graph. This is usually expressed in point slope form where:
	\begin{align*}
		m = \frac{y_2-y_1}{x_2-x_1}
	\end{align*}
	then the secant line is:
	\begin{align*}
		y-y_1 = m(x-x_1)
	\end{align*}
